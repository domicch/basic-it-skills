# Computer Skills for Everyone

Welcome to a practical guide for learning essential computer and technology skills. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your knowledge, these courses will help you use your computer, phone, and internet with confidence.

## What You'll Learn

This course covers a wide range of practical topics:
- **Internet Safety** — How to recognize and avoid scams and phishing
- **Password Security** — Creating strong passwords and keeping your accounts safe
- **Home Networks** — Understanding how your router and Wi-Fi work
- **Malware Protection** — Protecting your devices from viruses
- **Social Media** — Staying safe and private on social platforms
- **Mobile Phones** — Securing your smartphone
- **Online Shopping** — Shopping safely and protecting your payment information

## How to Use These Courses

1. **Start with what interests you** — All courses are independent and can be taken in any order
2. **Learn at your own pace** — Read through the lessons as slowly or quickly as you like
3. **Apply what you learn** — Practice the tips in your daily digital life
4. **Come back anytime** — Return to any lesson if you need a refresher
